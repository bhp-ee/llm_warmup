# 1. Fundamentals of LLMs & AI

## 1.1 What are Large Language Models?

Large Language Models (LLMs) are advanced AI systems trained on vast amounts of text data. They can understand and generate human-like text, making them powerful tools for various applications.

## 1.2 Key Concepts

- **Transformers:** The architecture behind modern LLMs
- **Attention Mechanism:** How models focus on relevant information
- **Pre-training & Fine-tuning:** Two-stage training approach
- **Tokenization:** Breaking text into processable units

## 1.3 Popular LLM Models

- GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer)
- BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers)
- Claude, Gemini, LLaMA, and others
