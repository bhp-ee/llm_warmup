## Chapter 5.3: Development Tools & APIs

### LLM APIs

#### [OpenAI API](https://platform.openai.com)
- **Models:** GPT-4, GPT-3.5, Embeddings, DALL-E
- **Pricing:** Pay-per-token
- **Features:** Chat, completion, fine-tuning
- **Rate limits:** Tiered based on usage
- **Best for:** Production applications

#### [Anthropic Claude](https://anthropic.com)
- **Models:** Claude 3 (Opus, Sonnet, Haiku)
- **Context:** Up to 200K tokens
- **Focus:** Safety and reliability
- **Features:** Vision, analysis, coding
- **Best for:** Long documents, safe outputs

#### [Google AI Studio](https://makersuite.google.com)
- **Model:** Gemini Pro, Gemini Ultra
- **Features:** Multimodal (text, image, video)
- **Pricing:** Free tier available
- **Integration:** Google Cloud
- **Best for:** Multimodal applications

#### [Cohere](https://cohere.com)
- **Models:** Command, Embed, Rerank
- **Focus:** Enterprise NLP
- **Features:** Multilingual support
- **Best for:** Business applications

### Vector Databases

#### [Pinecone](https://pinecone.io)
- **Type:** Managed vector database
- **Features:** Fast similarity search, filtering
- **Scaling:** Auto-scaling
- **Integration:** Easy SDKs
- **Pricing:** Generous free tier
- **Best for:** Production RAG systems

#### [Weaviate](https://weaviate.io)
- **Type:** Open-source vector database
- **Features:** GraphQL API, hybrid search
- **Modules:** Built-in ML models
- **Deployment:** Self-hosted or cloud
- **Best for:** Flexible, customizable solutions

#### Other Options
- **Chroma:** Lightweight, embedded
- **Qdrant:** High-performance, Rust-based
- **Milvus:** Distributed, scalable
- **FAISS:** Facebook AI Similarity Search (library)

### Experiment Tracking

#### [Weights & Biases](https://wandb.ai)
- **Features:** Experiment tracking, visualization
- **Sweeps:** Hyperparameter optimization
- **Artifacts:** Model versioning
- **Reports:** Share results
- **Integration:** PyTorch, TensorFlow, Keras
- **Pricing:** Free for personal use

#### [MLflow](https://mlflow.org)
- **Type:** Open-source platform
- **Components:** Tracking, projects, models, registry
- **Deployment:** Model serving
- **Best for:** End-to-end ML lifecycle
- **Integration:** Cloud-agnostic

#### Alternatives
- **Neptune.ai:** Metadata management
- **Comet.ml:** Experiment tracking
- **TensorBoard:** TensorFlow visualization
- **Aim:** Open-source tracking

## Chapter 5.5: Books & Reading Materials

### Beginner Books

- **"Hands-On Machine Learning"** by Aurélien Géron
  - Practical, code-focused
  - Covers scikit-learn and TensorFlow

- **"Deep Learning for Coders"** by Jeremy Howard & Sylvain Gugger
  - Fast.ai approach
  - Top-down learning

### Intermediate Books

- **"Deep Learning"** by Goodfellow, Bengio, Courville
  - Comprehensive theory
  - Mathematical foundations

- **"Natural Language Processing with Transformers"** by Tunstall, von Werra, Wolf
  - Modern NLP with Hugging Face
  - Practical transformer applications

### Advanced Books

- **"Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction"** by Sutton & Barto
  - RL fundamentals
  - Free online

- **"Probabilistic Machine Learning"** by Kevin Murphy
  - Advanced theory
  - Two volumes
